What is Oggy?

Oggy is an all-in-one search engine for the comparison of food offers on your favorite restaurants. It accumulates data for the comparison of best offers from the Food Giants like Zomato, Swiggy, Eazydiner, Dineout, and Magicpin. The users of oggy can compare the various offers at a restaurant provided by these Food delivery apps or Aggregators. Users will be able to compare the prices & offers at over 4000+ restaurants in Jaipur and can pick the best deals for them. Oggy already serves in 11+ cities as of now and spreading steadily.
